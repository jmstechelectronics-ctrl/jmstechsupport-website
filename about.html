Before starting JMS Tech Support, I spent over a decade working as an electronic gaming technician, maintaining and repairing complex electronic systems.

That background carries directly into the way I work today: methodical fault finding, practical repairs, and reliable troubleshooting for the everyday technology problems people actually need solved.

Now I help people around Yamba, Maclean and the Clarence Valley sort out the slow computers, Wi-Fi issues, printer problems, TV setup headaches, and security camera installs that can quickly become frustrating.
