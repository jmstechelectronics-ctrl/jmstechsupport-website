FAQ

Straight answers about local support, slow internet, cameras and service coverage.

Why is my internet slow at night?

Usually local Wi-Fi congestion, household usage peaks, or upstream provider limits. We test each layer and show you the bottleneck.

Can you fix Wi-Fi dead zones?

Yes. We optimise router placement and settings first, then add mesh where your layout needs wider coverage.

Do you support NBN troubleshooting?

Yes. We help isolate whether the issue is local hardware, Wi-Fi setup, or likely provider-side.

Can you also install security cameras?

Yes. We install and configure cameras with app setup, motion alerts and playback support.

Which areas do you cover?

Yamba, Maclean, Iluka, Townsend, Gulmarrad, Grafton and nearby Clarence Valley suburbs.
